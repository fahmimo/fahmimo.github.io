Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save a wallpaper to my device?

Once you find a wallpaper you like, tap the download or save icon. You will need to grant permission for the app to access your photo library.

How do I add quotes to my wallpaper?

In the wallpaper detail screen, you can select from various motivational quotes to overlay on the image before saving or setting it as your background.

The app isn't loading images, what should I do?

Please check your internet connection. If the problem persists, try restarting the app or clearing the cache in your device settings.

How do I manage my premium subscription?

You can manage your subscriptions directly through your Apple ID settings on your iOS device or through the "Manage Subscriptions" section in the app settings.
